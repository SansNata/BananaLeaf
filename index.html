About Us

Hello! My name is Raynathan, and I'm aspiring young export trader based in Banten, Indonesia. Inspired by the growing global demand for natural packaging and traditional food preparation, I started Banten Banana Leaf farmers with international buyers. Although I'm just starting, I'm committed to make Banten Banana Leaf goes all over the world and building strong partnership with farmers and providing consistent, export-ready banana leaves for markets abroad.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring the freshness of Indonesia’s banana leaves to the global market. Living in Banten, surrounded by banana plantations, we saw an untapped potential — not just for fruit, but for the leaf itself, valued in global culinary and packaging industries.

We started reaching out to local farmers in Pandeglang and Serang, learning about the varieties, the harvesting methods, and the challenges of maintaining quality. Step by step, we are building a supply chain that connects tradition with international standards. This is just the beginning — and we invite you to be part of our journey
